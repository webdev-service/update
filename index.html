The $PRO Token

$PRO tokens play a crucial role in the Prometheus network, featuring four primary functionalities:

(Prometheus)

Network Security Staking:

$PRO tokens can be staked to validators, enhancing the network's security. Stakers receive rewards as an incentive for their active participation.

Gas Fee Settlement:

These tokens are utilized for settling gas fees, a fundamental requirement for transaction execution and various network operations.

Facilitating On-Chain Transactions:

$PRO acts as the native asset for on-chain transactions, playing a vital role in the Prometheus economy.

Governance Participation:

Holding $PRO tokens grants users the privilege to participate in future governance decisions of the network.

The overall supply

$PRO is capped at 1 billion tokens, ensuring scarcity and economic stability within the Prometheus ecosystem.
